Biography

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). At present, I am working with Prof. Jiantao Pu on deep learning and medical image analysis.

Research Interests

My research is at the interface between medical imaging and machine learning, specifically computer-aided disease detection and diagnosis.
